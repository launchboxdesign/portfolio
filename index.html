Meet Jason

With over ten years of design and marketing experience in the financial sector, Jason has honed his skills solving branding and promotional problems. He also moonlights as a freelancer and he's worked with various clients from nonprofits and charter schools to start-ups and even a trucking company.

Jason likes his drinks cold, his lamb medium well and would love to hopefully, maybe, possibly, someday see the Memphis Grizzlies or Tennessee Titans win a championship. In his spare time, Jason can be found hanging out with his wife, Rhashad, and terror (sorry, terrier), Cooper.
