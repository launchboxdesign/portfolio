Miss Crumpy's Branding and Packaging

Restaurant

Faced with the challenge of penetrating an oversaturated hot wing market, the Atlanta-based wing spot approached us about refreshing their logo and brand identity. They wanted to update their current look but still reference their Memphis roots, while keeping a similar look and feel of the old logo. Known for their award-winning wings, hospitality, and delicious sauces, the appeal of Miss Crumpy’s stretches across generations and states alike.

I was thrilled when Miss Crumpy’s approached me about refreshing the look of its brand. I conducted a deep dive into the passion and love that makes the people behind the food and service so special. We developed an identity and packaging that did the brand justice.

Transportation FCU Promotional Collateral

Financial Institution

Transportation FCU, was in need of some new collateral and printed material for new members as well as internal staff. They wanted to update their new member direct mail onboarding pieces as well as some of the internal items they regularly disseminate to employees.

I worked with the Training and Finance departments to create brochures for the diversity training as well as the annual report. The Marketing department also collaborated to refresh the member onboarding mailers that have increased product penetration for new members by an average of 53% annually.

Hats and Heels, Inc.

Nonprofit Organization

Hats and Heels is a nonprofit in Washington, DC focused on helping young women find their purpose and seeks to make a lasting impact on teen girls through service and philanthropy. They needed a fresh look and compelling branding to appeal to young teen girls to capture the spirit of Hats and Heels mission.

The result was an eye-catching identity that helps the brand portray their passion to work with young teens girls and a vested interest in their success in life. Finally, I extended the visual system to some basic apparel as well.

Transportation FCU

Financial Institution

Transportation, for the most part, is your typical credit union. In an industry that is generally resistant to change, Transportation Federal Credit Union is making a push to bring our members up to date with technology.

With that in mind, I worked to update and redesign loading pages, icons and the general user interface of the mobile app. Now the interface is clean, minimal and members are able to choose the best options to fit their goals, while current members are smoothly guided through tasks as if they were visiting a physical branch—directly from any phone, tablet or laptop.

True Holiness Lighthouse Church

Faith-Based Nonprofit

True Holiness Lighthouse Church is a church in Memphis, dedicated to helping people find salvation through faith in Jesus Christ, and through serving both church and community. The Leadership Team wanted a brand identity that went with the feel of their church and congregation.

Through research and conversations with the Leadership Team, I was able to define some of the unique elements of this particular church and congregation that became the building blocks for a new brand. The lighthouse was the key symbolism that aligned with their calling and mission as a church.

D and R Trucking, LLC

Frieght Transport Company

D and R Trucking is a small trucking company in Memphis that transports freight around the metro area. I was asked to create a log that would inspire confidence in D and R trucking and their capacity to transport your freight safely to the desired destination. The design had to appeal to the trucking community and approachable by other industries at the same time.

In collaboration with some of the company leadership, I was able to design a logo that serves as the foundation of their new look. The simplicity of the highway sign shape along with the road and north-star symbolism implies confidence that D and R knows the transport business. The mark scales and displays well on various media.
